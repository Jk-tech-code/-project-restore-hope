Glorious Group of Schools

A place of hope and opportunity for the children of Mukuru, Kenya.

A Place of Hope and Opportunity

Glorious Group of Schools serves children living in Mukuru wa Njenga and surrounding communities. The school provides quality education, daily meals, Christian values, academic support, and safe learning environments.

The school has become a beacon of hope for children whose families face significant economic challenges.

Support a Child

Educational Achievement

In 2019, Glorious Group of Schools competed against 221 schools in Nairobi and emerged as the top-performing school in the city.

This achievement demonstrates what is possible when children are given access to education, encouragement, and opportunity.

What We Provide

Quality Education

Comprehensive education from nursery through secondary school, preparing students for a bright future.

Daily Meals

Every student receives two nutritious meals each day, ensuring they can learn without hunger.

Academic Support

Dedicated teachers and staff provide individualized attention and support to help every child succeed.

Safe Environment

A secure and nurturing environment where children can learn, play, and grow with dignity.

Christian Values

Spiritual guidance and moral education grounded in Christian principles and community.

Excellence

Top-ranked school in Nairobi (2019), proving that every child can achieve greatness with opportunity.
