Our Programs

Comprehensive support for children and families in Mukuru through education, nutrition, orphan care, and community development.

Education Support

At the heart of Project Restore Hope is our commitment to education. We provide quality education to over 700 students at Glorious Group of Schools in Mukuru, giving children a path out of poverty through learning.

Each student receives two daily meals, school supplies, uniforms, and access to trained teachers who are dedicated to their success. Our academic track record speaks for itself, with students consistently ranking among the top in Nairobi.

Orphan Support

We currently support 70 orphaned children with comprehensive care that goes beyond the classroom. Our orphan support program provides shelter, education, healthcare, and a loving community where every child can thrive.

Each child is placed in a nurturing family environment, receiving the emotional support and stability they need to heal and grow. We are committed to walking alongside them every step of the way.

Our work extends beyond the school gates. Through church ministry and family support programs, we engage with the broader Mukuru community to address the root causes of poverty and bring holistic transformation.

We provide family counseling, parent workshops, community health screenings, and spiritual support â€” because restoring hope means strengthening the entire community.

Healthcare Outreach

Healthcare outreach remains an important part of our commitment to community well-being. Rooted in the medical mission work that first brought Dr. Mitchelle Scott to Mukuru in 2006, we seek to promote healthier lives through education, awareness, partnerships, and support initiatives.

Our focus areas include community health education, preventive healthcare awareness, medical mission partnerships, health screenings, child wellness support, and hygiene education. We also offer a Digital Health Education & Outreach Initiative that provides students with practical digital skills while creating economic opportunities through referral-based outreach.

Technology & Skills Development

We believe in preparing students for the future. Our technology program provides computer training, website design skills, and digital literacy education that equips students with the tools they need to succeed in a modern world.

From basic computer skills to advanced digital courses, we are bridging the digital divide and opening doors to new opportunities for our students and the wider community.
