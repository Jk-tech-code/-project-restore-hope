Volunteer With Us

Join us in making a difference in the lives of children and families in Mukuru, Kenya.

Ways to Serve

Mission Trips

Visit Mukuru and serve alongside our dedicated staff. Whether it's teaching, construction, or community outreach, your hands-on involvement makes a tangible difference in the lives of children and families.

Apply Now

Skills-Based Volunteering

Share your professional expertise in education, technology, healthcare, or other fields. Your skills can help build capacity, train local staff, and create sustainable solutions for the community.

Apply Now

Prayer & Advocacy

Support our work from home through faithful prayer and raising awareness. Become an advocate for the children of Mukuru by sharing our story with your church, community, and social networks.

Get Started

How to Apply

  1. 1. Submit Interest

    Fill out the application form below with your details and area of interest. Our volunteer coordinator will review your submission.

  2. 2. Interview

    We'll schedule a conversation to learn more about you, your skills, and how you can best contribute to our mission.

  3. 3. Training

    Attend orientation and training sessions to prepare for your role, whether serving locally or traveling to Mukuru.

  4. 4. Placement

    You'll be matched with a role that fits your gifts and availability, with ongoing support from our team.
