Partners & Sponsors

We are grateful for the organizations, churches, and individuals who partner with us to restore hope in Mukuru.

Our partners make everything possible. Through their generous support, we are able to provide education, meals, medical care, and hope to the children and families of Mukuru. Partnership is at the heart of our mission, and we treasure every relationship that helps us build a brighter future.
