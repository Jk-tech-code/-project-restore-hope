Our Impact

Transforming lives in Mukuru through education, nutrition, and community support since 2006.

When we began there were only 60 students. Today we support over 800 students, 70 orphans, and approximately 50 staff members while providing education and two meals a day for students and staff.

How We Make a Difference

Education

Providing quality education to hundreds of children each year at Glorious Group of Schools, from nursery through secondary school.

Nutrition

Providing two meals every day to students and staff, ensuring that no child learns on an empty stomach.

Orphan Support

Supporting approximately 70 orphaned children through education, care, and a loving community environment.

Employment

Supporting approximately 50 staff members through the school and ministry, providing stable employment in the community.

Community Development

Working toward sustainable solutions that create opportunities for long-term independence and community growth.

Academic Excellence

In 2019, Glorious Group of Schools was ranked the top school in Nairobi, beating 221 other schools.
