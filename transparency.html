Financial Transparency

Project Restore Hope is committed to responsible stewardship, accountability, and transparency in the management of all donations and resources entrusted to the organization.

We believe donors, partners, sponsors, and supporters deserve clear information about how funds are used to support the children, school, church, and community programs in Mukuru, Kenya. Every contribution is used effectively to maximize impact for the children and families we serve.

How Donations Are Used

Your donations directly support life-changing programs and initiatives in Mukuru, including:

  • Student education and school fees
  • School feeding programs (two meals daily)
  • Orphan support and care
  • Teacher and staff salaries

  • School infrastructure improvements
  • Community outreach initiatives
  • Technology and skills development programs
  • Future building construction projects

Accountability

Project Restore Hope is committed to ensuring that every contribution is used effectively to maximize impact for the children and families we serve. We strive to maintain the highest standards of transparency, integrity, and stewardship in all areas of our work. Our financial records are audited annually and are available upon request.

Impact Reporting

We track and share measurable results to demonstrate the impact of your support:

  • 700+ students supported annually
  • 70 orphans receiving full care
  • 2 meals provided daily to students and staff

  • #1 ranked school in Nairobi (2019)
  • Infrastructure developments underway
  • Community impact across Mukuru
