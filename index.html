How We Help

Every day, we provide education, nutrition, healthcare, and community support to nearly 800 children and their families in Mukuru.

Quality Education

Glorious Group of Schools provides pre-primary, primary, and secondary education to nearly 800 students, ranked #1 in Nairobi in 2019.

Daily Nutrition

We serve two nutritious meals every school day to every student and staff member, ensuring no child tries to learn on an empty stomach.

Our Journey Since 2006

From humble beginnings with 60 students to a top-ranked school serving nearly 800 children.

2006

School Founded

Glorious Group of Schools opens its doors with just 60 students in Mukuru, providing free education to children who otherwise could not afford it.

2012

Feeding Program Launched

PRH begins providing daily meals to students, ensuring that hunger is no longer a barrier to learning.

2019

#1 School in Nairobi

Glorious Group of Schools is ranked the top school out of 221 in Nairobi, proving that excellence thrives in Mukuru.

2026

800 Students & Growing

Today we serve nearly 800 students, support 70 orphans, employ 50 staff, and continue expanding our facilities and programs.
