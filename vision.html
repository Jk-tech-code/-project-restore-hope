Building for the Next Generation

Our vision for a new facility that will transform education and create sustainable opportunities for the children of Mukuru.

The Need for a New Building

The current school and church facility is small and has undergone numerous repairs over the years. The building is constructed of corrugated tin, mud, and wood and can no longer adequately support the growing needs of the community.

Project Restore Hope is currently planning and raising funds for the construction of a new facility.

The Proposed Building Will

Safer Learning Environment

A secure, modern four-story building that provides a safe space for children to learn and grow.

Expanded Classroom Capacity

More classrooms to accommodate the growing number of students and reduce overcrowding.

Improved Educational Facilities

Better facilities including a library, computer lab, and dedicated spaces for learning and development.

Technology Training

A dedicated space for computer programming, website design, and digital skills training.

Internet-Based Employment

Creating opportunities for community members to earn income through online work and digital entrepreneurship.

Long-Term Sustainability

Building a foundation for lasting community development and economic independence.

Help Us Build the Future

The children and families of Mukuru deserve a safe, modern facility where they can learn, grow, and build a better future. We cannot do this alone and would greatly appreciate your help.
