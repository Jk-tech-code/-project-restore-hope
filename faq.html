Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Project Restore Hope.

Donations & Sponsorship

How are my donations used?+

Your donations go directly toward our core programs: education, daily nutrition, orphan support, and community outreach in Mukuru. We maintain strict financial accountability to ensure maximum impact.

Is my donation tax-deductible?+

Yes, Project Restore Hope is a registered nonprofit organization, and donations are tax-deductible to the extent allowed by law. You will receive a receipt for your contribution.

Can I sponsor a child?+

Yes, you can sponsor a child through our child sponsorship program. Your support provides education, meals, and care for a specific child. Contact us for more information on how to get started.

How do I get a receipt?+

Receipts are automatically sent via email for online donations. If you donate by other means, please contact us and we will provide a receipt promptly.

Our Mission

What does Project Restore Hope do?+

We are a nonprofit organization dedicated to restoring hope, transforming lives, and building futures for children and families in Mukuru, Kenya. We run a school, feeding program, orphan support, and community development initiatives.

Where is Mukuru?+

Mukuru is a large informal settlement (slum) located in Nairobi, Kenya. It is home to hundreds of thousands of people facing extreme poverty, lack of access to education, and food insecurity.

Who leads the organization?+

Project Restore Hope is led by a dedicated team of local and international leaders committed to serving the community of Mukuru with integrity and compassion. Visit our About page for more details.

Volunteering

How can I volunteer?+

You can volunteer locally or remotely. We welcome volunteers for teaching, fundraising, administrative support, and special projects. Fill out our volunteer form to get started.

Are there mission trips?+

Yes, we organize mission trips to Mukuru where volunteers can serve alongside our team. Trips typically involve working at the school, serving meals, and participating in community outreach.

What skills are needed?+

We need a variety of skills including teaching, construction, medical, administrative, fundraising, and communications. No matter your background, there is a way to contribute.

The Building Project

Why is a new building needed?+

Our current facility is no longer adequate to serve the growing number of children in need. A new building will provide safe classrooms, a proper kitchen, and space for expanded programs.

How much will it cost?+

The total cost of the building project is estimated based on construction needs in Nairobi. We are actively fundraising and provide updates on our progress through our news page and reports.

How can I contribute to the building fund?+

You can contribute through our donate page, designate your gift to the building fund, or organize a fundraiser. Every donation, large or small, brings us closer to our goal.

Still Have Questions?

Contact us and we will be happy to help.
